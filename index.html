DOCU — MY WORLD
Where my heart feels at home.
docu, tum meri zindagi ka sabse khoobsurat hissa ho, aur ye baat main kisi khaas din par nahi, dil se maanta hoon. Tumhari muskurahat, tumhari baatein, aur tumhara hona — ye sab milkar meri duniya ko thoda aur pyara bana dete hain. Jab main tumhare baare me sochta hoon, toh koi confusion nahi hota, koi doubt nahi hota. Bas ek simple sa ehsaas hota hai — ke main tumhare saath rehna chahta hoon. Main tumse wada karta hoon ke pyaar sirf lafzon me nahi, apni har chhoti-badi koshish me dikhane ki koshish karunga. Tum meri choice ho, meri khushi ho, aur meri duniya ho.
Mohabbat shor nahi karti, wo chup-chaap dil me ghar bana leti hai. Tum waise hi mere dil me rehti ho.
— mumu
